Feral Ranch

Forward

The rough fabric covering my eyes could not hide the fact that I was presently being dragged across a pine forest floor.

As if I would be able to see anyway, the air's chill was a continuous reminder that it was still night and the sun's warmth was far, far, away.

My foggy mind began to return to me and I built up the courage to scream for help.

"Quiet yourself, or I'll stop and kill you here" my assailant howled, exasperatedly.

"If not me, them..." he then muttered.

I was confused, but not scared. I knew who I was dealing with, this was my mark. I had been hunting him for weeks, a man living on the outskirts of a wealthy rancher's land.
He was a petty thief accused of stealing a few goats, and I was hired to make an example of him.
