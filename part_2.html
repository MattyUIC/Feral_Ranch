Feral Ranch

Him

"You know you have now committed a federal offense. I'm a Marshal." I lied.

He said nothing.

"There is a warrant for your arrest issued by the owner of this ranch, Martin Ferrell." I continued.

He grunted and dropped me. Frantically, I began kicking my legs to try and get away, but his heavy foot stomped down on my sternum.
I gasped and tried to catch my breath but was knocked unconscious again.

I awoke to screams. Guttural cries faded to drowning gurgles, then silence and the sounds of a crackling fire.

Adrenaline coursed through my veins and I began to struggle. I quickly realized that now my hands were tied behind my back, to a tree.

The sounds of snapping twigs and russling foliage triggered a new wave of anxiety and I redoubled my efforts to free myself. Out of all other options, I knew what I had to do.
